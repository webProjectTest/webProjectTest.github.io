About Us

We are a family-owned take-out restaurant serving quality Chinese Food in the Upper Cape since 2006!

Previously known as Rainbow Kitchen inside the Cape Cod Outlet Mall. Our independent location has been open in Sagamore Beach since 2011.

We are dedicated to use only fresh and quality ingredients and offer an extensive selection of dishes with daily lunch and all day specials!
